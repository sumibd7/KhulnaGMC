About Us

Welcome To Our College

Higher education in Bangladesh is a unique and pioneering institution. In eighty years, the institution has been playing a constant role in the development of talent, thinking and human resources of the nation. The institution itself has become an integral part of our national history, being inextricably linked with the socio-economic and cultural development of the nation. As many thinkers, scholars and art teachers of the country have enlightened the institution, similarly many of the students here have left their mark in the national and international arena. The institution is also a proud participant in every glorious chapter of our national history including the language movement and the great liberation war. Government Haji Muhammad Mohsin College continues to carry on its legacy of glory despite various constraints. The college is relentless in improving the quality of education in the vein of glorious tradition
